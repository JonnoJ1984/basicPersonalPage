Jonathan Jacka

I am a teacher.

I am committed to developing learning experiences and environments with creative approaches to rigor, equity, and positive expectations for students of all backgrounds in promoting critical thinking habits.

My mission:

to turn answer-getters

into problem-solvers

To me, meaningful learning is:

  • founded on Common Core Standards and Universal Design for Learning principles.
  • administered with continuous data collection and analysis to drive instruction.
  • developed with assessment methods that provide multiple paths to show mastery.
  • a constant celebration student successes and growth.
  • intentionally differentiated according to learners' diverse social, cultural, and multilingual needs.
  • integrated with technology only as a meaningful aid to learning.
  • created and grown in collaboration with others.
  • managed with clarity, encouragement, and consistency.
  • committed to an anti-racist agenda.

Some skills I have include:

A deep knowledge of the concepts and concerns of Computer Science, with proficiency in Java, Python, HTML, CSS, Dart, and JavaScript.

Development and implementation of high-leverage instructional strategies to promote collaborative learning and student engagement.

A dedication to positive collaboration between colleagues as one of the most effective tools to produce high-quality work and continued motivation.

I strive to balance learning in

  • rich content
  • deep inquiry
  • meaningful equity
